Our Story

We started STILLY because we were tired of choosing between great taste and feeling great. Every cocktail on the market was either loaded with sugar and artificial ingredients, or tasted like compromise.

So we created something better: premium vodka meets 100% natural fruit juice. Real ingredients, real flavor, and only 110 calories. No artificial sweeteners. No guilt. No compromise.

STILLY is for the people who want to enjoy life — at the beach, on the golf course, at the rooftop party, or just a Tuesday. Made with care, crafted for fun.
